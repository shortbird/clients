Stop Jumping Through Hoops.
Start Building Your Future.

Trailhead School was founded on a simple idea: education should be built around a student's passions, not a standardized curriculum. We empower students by trusting them to direct their own learning, with expert teachers helping them build real skills for the future.

How It Works

1. Design Your MAP

You'll partner one-on-one with one of our expert teachers to define a high-level goal, broken down into smaller, achievable milestones.

2. Build & Learn

Work through your milestones at your own pace, with constant support from our team of specialist educators. You'll build real skills by doing real work.

3. Showcase Success

Present your completed work at our community Showcase Nights to build a tangible portfolio that is ready to impress employers and investors.

Meet Your Teachers

With over 20 years of combined experience, our founders created Trailhead out of a frustration with the restrictive public school system. Their mission is to empower students by trusting them to direct their own learning.

Tanner Bowman

A Computer Science teacher at American Fork High School, Tanner's doctoral research focused on increasing student motivation by giving them greater autonomy over their learning.

Calvin Barnum

As the marketing, business, and entrepreneurship teacher at American Fork High School, Calvin specializes in project-based learning to help students build tangible businesses and portfolios.
